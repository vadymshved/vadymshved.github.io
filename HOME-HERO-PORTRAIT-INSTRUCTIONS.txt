HOME HERO PORTRAIT — HOW TO REPLACE

Current portrait file:
assets/images/gallery/portrait/hero-portrait.svg

You can replace it with your own photo in 2 ways.

OPTION 1 — EASIEST
1. Prepare your portrait image.
2. Rename it to:
   hero-portrait.jpg
   or hero-portrait.png
   or hero-portrait.webp
3. Put it into:
   assets/images/gallery/portrait/
4. Open this file:
   assets/data/site-media.json
5. Change:
   "src": "assets/images/gallery/portrait/hero-portrait.svg"
   to for example:
   "src": "assets/images/gallery/portrait/hero-portrait.jpg"

OPTION 2 — KEEP YOUR OWN FILE NAME
1. Put your image into:
   assets/images/gallery/portrait/
2. Open:
   assets/data/site-media.json
3. Change the "src" field to your file name.
   Example:
   "src": "assets/images/gallery/portrait/my-portrait-2026.jpg"

RECOMMENDED IMAGE FORMAT
- vertical portrait photo
- JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- recommended minimum width: 1400 px
- recommended aspect: portrait photo with some free space around head and shoulders

CURRENT CROPPING BEHAVIOR
- the image fills the whole hero frame automatically
- it uses cover mode
- it is centered
- the visible focus is slightly shifted upward to better frame the face

IF YOU WANT TO ADJUST CROPPING LATER
Open:
assets/css/multipage.css

Find:
.portrait-box--hero .portrait-box-image

Current rule:
object-position: center 24%;

Examples:
- center 20%  -> shifts image a bit higher
- center 30%  -> shifts image a bit lower
- center center -> perfect center crop

IMPORTANT
You do NOT need to edit index.html every time.
For future portrait changes, only update:
- the image file in assets/images/gallery/portrait/
- and, if needed, the src path in assets/data/site-media.json
